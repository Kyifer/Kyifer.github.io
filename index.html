Manchester is Full of People.
Let's Help You Find Yours.

BeSocia is a community dedicated to combating loneliness and creating genuine friendships. We host regular, relaxed events designed to make it easy to connect with others in a safe and welcoming environment.

Start Connecting Today

What We're All About

Real-World Events

From casual pub meets and board game nights to city walks and coffee mornings, our focus is on getting people together in person to build real connections.

A Safe & Welcoming Space

Our community is built on a foundation of respect and kindness. Our comprehensive Code of Conduct and dedicated moderation team ensure a safe environment for all.

Community-Driven

BeSocia is shaped by its members. Through feedback, suggestions, and our volunteer program, everyone has the opportunity to help build the community they want to be a part of.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Our volunteers are the heart of BeSocia. If you're passionate about our mission and want to develop your skills, we have a role for you.
