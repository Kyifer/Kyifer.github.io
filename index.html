Manchester is Full of People.
Let's Help You Find Yours.

BeSocia is a community dedicated to combating loneliness and creating genuine friendships. We host regular, relaxed events designed to make it easy to connect with others in a safe and welcoming environment.

Start Connecting Today

Our Story: More Than Just a Group

We started with a simple problem: in a city of millions, it can be incredibly hard to find your people. We wanted to change that.

Our Mission: A Cure for Loneliness

Our core mission is to create a safe, inclusive, and welcoming environment where individuals in Greater Manchester can connect, build genuine friendships, and engage in meaningful social activities. We're here to be the antidote to the social isolation that modern life can bring.

Our Vision: A Connected City

We envision a Manchester where nobody feels they have to face the city alone. A community where shared experiences forge strong bonds, and every member feels a true sense of belonging, support, and empowerment.

What to Expect When You Join

We're all about low-pressure situations and high-quality connections.

Relaxed Events

No awkward networking or forced fun. Just casual meetups like pub quizzes, board games, and coffee chats where conversations happen naturally.

A Culture of Kindness

Our community is governed by a strict Code of Conduct that prioritizes safety, respect, and compassion. We have zero tolerance for harassment or bigotry.

An Active Online Hub

Our Discord server is the heart of our community. It's where we plan events, share interests, and keep the conversation going between meetups.

Ready to Make a Difference?

Our volunteers are the heart of BeSocia. If you're passionate about our mission and want to develop your skills, we have a role for you.
