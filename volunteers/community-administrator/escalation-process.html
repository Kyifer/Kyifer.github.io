Escalation Process

Level 1: Initial Response

For minor issues, provide an initial response based on the community guidelines. Document the issue and the action taken in the admin log.

Level 2: Senior Administrator Review

If an issue cannot be resolved at Level 1 or involves a sensitive matter, escalate it to a Senior Administrator. Provide all relevant documentation and context.

Level 3: Leadership Team Intervention

For serious violations or legal concerns, the issue must be escalated to the Leadership Team immediately. Do not attempt to handle these issues on your own.
