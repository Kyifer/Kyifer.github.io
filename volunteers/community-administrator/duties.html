Core Duties

Member Support

Provide timely and helpful responses to member inquiries through our support channels. Your goal is to ensure every member feels heard and supported.

Content Moderation

Review user-generated content to ensure it complies with our community guidelines. This includes posts, comments, and direct messages.

Onboarding New Members

Welcome new members to the community, guide them through the getting started process, and answer any initial questions they may have.
