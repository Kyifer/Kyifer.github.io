Event Best Practices

Start Planning Early

Give yourself plenty of time to plan. The earlier you start, the more options you will have for venues and vendors, and the less stressful the process will be.

Communicate Clearly

Maintain clear and consistent communication with the leadership team, other volunteers, and event attendees. Use the community's communication channels effectively.

Conduct a Post-Event Review

After the event, gather feedback from attendees and volunteers. Conduct a review to identify what went well and what could be improved for next time. Share your findings with the leadership team.
