Core Responsibilities

Event Conception and Planning

You are responsible for developing event concepts that align with the community's goals, and for planning all aspects of the event from venue and vendors to scheduling and logistics.

Budget Management

You must create and manage the event budget, ensuring all expenses are tracked and approved. You are accountable for keeping the event within its allocated budget.

Volunteer Coordination

You will be the main point of contact for other volunteers helping with the event. This includes delegating tasks, providing clear instructions, and ensuring everyone works together effectively.
