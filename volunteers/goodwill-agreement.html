Volunteer Goodwill Agreement

Our Commitment

This document outlines the shared understanding between the community and its volunteers. It is not a legally binding contract but a statement of mutual trust and respect. We commit to providing a supportive, safe, and rewarding environment for our volunteers.

Your Commitment

As a volunteer, you agree to perform your duties to the best of your ability, to adhere to the community's policies and procedures, and to represent our community in a positive and professional manner. Your time and effort are deeply valued.

Acknowledgement

By proceeding with your volunteer duties, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this goodwill agreement. We look forward to working with you to make our community a better place.
