Brand Guidelines

Logo Usage

Our logo should always be displayed with clear space and never be altered. Please use the approved logo files available in the Tools & Assets section.

Tone of Voice

Our tone is welcoming, inclusive, and positive. We aim to be helpful and encouraging in all our communications. Avoid jargon and be clear and concise.

Visual Style

Our visual style is modern, clean, and uses the color palette defined in our brand kit. All promotional materials should adhere to this style for consistency.
