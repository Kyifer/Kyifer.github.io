Volunteer Handbook

Welcome & Mission

This handbook is your guide to being a successful and impactful volunteer. Our mission is to build a vibrant, supportive, and engaging community, and your contribution is vital. We are thrilled to have you on the team.

General Policies

This section outlines our core policies, including our code of conduct, communication guidelines, and data privacy rules. Adherence to these policies ensures a safe and professional environment for all members and volunteers.
