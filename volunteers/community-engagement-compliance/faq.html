Frequently Asked Questions

What constitutes a conflict of interest?

A conflict of interest occurs when a personal interest or relationship could potentially influence your decisions as a volunteer. Always disclose potential conflicts to the Volunteer Coordinator.

How do I report a safety concern?

All safety concerns should be reported immediately to the designated safety officer for the event or to the Volunteer Coordinator. Use the incident report form available in the resources section.

Are there any restrictions on fundraising?

Yes, all fundraising activities must be pre-approved by the Leadership team to ensure they align with our mission and comply with local regulations. Please submit a fundraising proposal before you begin.
